\[I'=\frac{m_1R^2}{2}\]
\[\delta I' = \frac{\partial I'}{\partial m_1} \delta m_1 + \frac{\partial I'}{\partial R} \delta R=R \left ( \frac{R \delta m}{2} + m_1 \delta R \right )\]
